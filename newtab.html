Profile

  • Move your cursor across the canvas — it draws.
  • Leave it still for a second and the mandala draws itself, restyling as it goes.
  • Everything below changes the art live.
  • Press H to hide everything but the canvas. S saves a PNG, C clears, R rolls a random look.
  • Symmetry style changes the whole geometry — try Kaleidoscope, Spiral shells, or Tiled grid.
  • Pick the Custom palette to blend strokes through your own colors — or let Time of day / Match my weather choose for you.
  • Silk wisps paints smoke that keeps drifting after your stroke — loveliest with the Permanent or Cycle trail.
  • ✨ Today's mandala is seeded by the date — same for everyone, new every day.
  • Save current look keeps your favourite settings as your own preset.
  • Copy my look turns your settings into text you can send to a friend — they hit Paste a look and draw with it. Ambient settings share the same way from their tab.
  • In Ambient, Gallery mode builds a piece, fades it out, and begins a new one.
  • Hold Shift and drag to tilt the camera in 3D.
  • Hold Shift and double-click to reset the camera.
  • Right-click a quick link to remove it.
